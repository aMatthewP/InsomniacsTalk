About Us

We are Lifelabs group 3 from B02-Passion-10-2, we are livestreamers who aim to promote healthy sleep through livestreaming our disscussions about sleep deprivation and it's effects. We will discuss the personal experiences of fellow APEC students with sleep deprivation, how it has affected them, and how they cope with. We highly encourage everyone who is experiencing sleep deprivation to watch our streams to learn about the dangers of the lack of sleep and how this may cause changes to your body. Getting a good night's rest is often an overlooked part of health and we believe that it should not be overlooked and should be taken seriously.
